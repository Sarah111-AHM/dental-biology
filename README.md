# Biology Study Notes

## About this repository
This repository contains biology study notes for university students  
The notes are written in simple clear English  
Each topic is explained step by step for real understanding  
The content follows lectures textbooks and exam questions  

This repository is built during daily study  
It shows learning progress and concept building

ATP structure and function  
Exergonic and endergonic reactions  
Catabolic and anabolic pathways  

Chapter 12 and 13 Genetics Basics  
Chromosomes and inheritance  
Recombination  
Genetic variation  
Basic genetics concepts related to meiosis  

Chapter 13 Meiosis and Sexual Life Cycles  
Meiosis I and Meiosis II  
Homologous chromosomes  
Crossing over  
Independent assortment  

Chapter 40 Energy and Thermodynamics  
First law of thermodynamics  
Second law of thermodynamics  
Open and closed systems  
Energy flow in living organisms  

Chapter 40 Animal Form and Function  
Energy use in animals  
Thermoregulation  
Endotherms and ectotherms  
Evaporative cooling
